Computer Generated Illustrations

Creating 3D computer generated interior mock-ups and floor plans for a series of international Visa Centre’s.

The visual’s needed to be developed in a modular way, allowing there to be flexibility with rolling the designs out across multiple sites’ and geographical locations.

The Illustrations and plans were created using templates for an interchangeable approach on colours, images and layouts.

Initial ideas were sketched by hand – these were then developed into fully rendered 3D outcomes using computer generated software techniques and processes.
