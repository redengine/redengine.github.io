Craig started his career working for independent paper merchants.

In 2003 he moved into printing joining Eurographics who represented several European based web offset and gravure printers - he then continued with Thijsen Media Group, a Dutch web offset printer who bought Eurographics.

He joined York and Pindar in 2013, where he worked for 4 years.

And now working with Red Engine, he's looking forward to offering his portfolio of clients all aspects of printing, as well as photography, video and publishing.
