It was suggested I could bring a Valentines' theme into this month's blog. But me being me I couldn't bring myself to go down a predictable or sugar-coated route - even a brief look at the history of the day didn't get me inspired either.

So, I decided to look at the celebrated design partnership of Charles and Ray Eames - my tenuous romantic link is with them being a couple as well as business partners - my take on the Valentines' theme!!!



Image: Eames House, Pacific Palisades ©Danielschreurs/Dreamstime.com

I'm a big admirer of their work across architecture, graphics, furniture and education... They also used film and photography extensively and there were some lovely examples of both at the Barbican recently. I was particularly taken with the black and white images documenting the construction of their Los Angeles house, accompanied by some exquisite architectural drawings. But I just love Modernist Architecture anyway.

One of my favourite images of Charles and Ray Eames can be seen here

My lasting impression was of how productive and creative they were - and then the attention to detail, with layouts for graphic design, magazine covers and print. It's a great example of problem solving and teamwork and the power and creative genius that can be produced by creative collaboration - a lifetime of living and designing together. The power of sharing!!!

Love this Charles Eames quote "Eventually everything connects - people, ideas, objects. The quality of the connections is the key to quality per se."
